My experience



Web Design/Development

Currently I am versed in the programming languages HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, PHP, and MySQL. When it comes to frameworks I have experience with React, VueJS, jQuery, Bootstrap, Materialize, and Pure. Aside from languages and frameworks I also have experience working with Terminal and Apache.

Graphic Design

On the graphics side I have experience working with the Adobe Suite products such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Lightroom, and Experience Design. I utilize these programs mainly to create content such as logos and wire frames for myself or clients that I can use on web pages.

Freelance

Putting a big smile on someone's face never gets old which is one of my main motivations for freelancing. Through freelancing I have the opportunity to meet some really great people. Most of my freelance projects utilize Wordpress to design and deliver a finished product to the client. In past projects I have used plugins such as WooCommmerce, Yoast SEO, Divi, and Events Calender to name a few. Aside from the design work I also provide support to help clients migrate their websites from one domain provider to a different one.




A Little About Me

My name is Frank Schiller. Growing up I've always had a passion for art and graphics. I found artworks fascinating and art as a digital medium even more so. Originally this led me down the path to become a graphic designer. That changed halfway through my college career when I fell in love with the internet and began to learn how beautiful of an art form creating websites really is. I'm currently a senior Web Design/Development major at the University of Central Florida where I continue to learn more everyday. Aside from the serious stuff I'm a fitness advocate and an aspiring Pokemon trainer.
